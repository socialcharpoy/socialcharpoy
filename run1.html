Why

Villages in India are dotted with charpoys - traditional, sturdy woven beds - that serve a uniquely social function. The charpoy is the place where the village folk huddle, debate their most pressing issues, and emerge with unique solutions to their problems . Like the village charpoy, ‘social charpoy’ is a space for converging dialogues around the development sector. Our focus is the use of tech to solve urgent problems in education, poverty, hunger, health, climate change, gender equality, water, sanitation, energy, environment and social justice.

How

Every fortnight we bring narratives from folks passionate about bringing technology solutions to solving some of the biggest, most urgent problems in the development sector in India. We do this through the medium of engaging podcasts and intelligent data stories.
